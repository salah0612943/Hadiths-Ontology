Sanad

Sanad is coming from Arabic sanad means "support" in Islam. Therefore, it is considered as a list from narrators who narrative Hadith of a statement, action, or approbation of Mohammad, one of his Companions (Sahabah), or a later authority (tabi), its reliability determines the validity of a Hadith.

Al-Bukhari: Tell us Abdullah ibn Yusuf, told us Malik about son of Shihab about Anas bin Malik - The Messenger of Allaah (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) said: "Do not oppress, do not envy, do not persecute, and be the servants of Allah and our brothers. It is not permissible for a Muslim to abandon his brother over three nights."

The men of support: Abdullah bin Yusuf, Malik, Ibn Shihab, Anas bin Malik.
