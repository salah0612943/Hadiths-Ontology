Entailment

Entailment is the relationship between two sentences, where the reality of one sentence (A) requires the reality of the sentence (B).

Example : Abdullah bin Masud (May Allah be pleased with him) reported: The Prophet (PBUH) said, "Truth leads to piety and piety leads to Jannah. A man persists in speaking the truth till he is enrolled with Allah as a truthful. Falsehood leads to vice and vice leads to the Fire (Hell), and a person persists on telling lies until he is enrolled as a liar"
